# Content publication policy v0.1

A project is eligible only when it is non-synthetic, public, approved or published, category-valid, paired with exactly one companion, provenance-reviewed, rights-safe, media-accessible, link-safe, limitation-aware, relation-safe, free of private paths/URLs, and free of validation blocks. Eligibility is computed; authors do not set it.

Fixtures are always `synthetic: true`, `visibility: private`, `noindex: true`, and not featured. The expected fixture-eligible count is zero. Draft/private records may omit final SEO, public links, complete media, verified evidence, and final review, but never contain secrets, credentials, private paths, unsafe URLs, or fabricated outcomes.

Publishable rights are owned, licensed, permission-granted, or public-domain. Synthetic rights are suitable only for fixtures. Public available media cannot use unknown/private/unavailable rights. Archived means preserved, not deleted; archive date, maintenance, releases, and superseding relations remain inspectable.
