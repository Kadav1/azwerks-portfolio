# Art project world v0.1

## Purpose and hierarchy

Art pages prioritize the artwork encounter, fidelity, ratio, medium, dimensions, period, reflection, process, rights, and provenance. The production order places process, limitations, and provenance directly after narrative; evidence, relations, links, and releases remain available when meaningful.

The quiet shell and media-led layout use the neutral theme profile. The stage may exceed narrative width, while reflection and provenance use a quieter centered measure. Metadata and evidence density are quiet, not absent.

## Media, proof, and fallbacks

Artwork is never cropped or tinted. It uses the governed neutral art surface, preserves intrinsic ratio, and keeps caption and credit adjacent. There is no museum frame, wall shadow, spotlight, gallery theater, hover-only inspection, or autoplay.

When artwork is unavailable, the shared dignified text-led state appears without invented imagery. Rights and provenance remain explicit. Dark and light modes are equally supported; forced colors preserves content, print retains documentary meaning, and no JavaScript is required. Authored marks are Level 0 and normally prohibited. Portrait, landscape, square, and no-image art fixtures are mandatory checks.
